What is Fetchr?

Fetchr is a technology solution that solves the problem of no physical addresses in emerging markets, and eliminates the need for annoying phone calls asking for directions.

How does it work?

The Fetchr app uses your phone as a GPS location. With our app, we can locate your exact location, allowing us to deliver packages to you faster and more efficiently.

How much does a standard delivery cost?

A standard delivery is 30 AED for a package under 3kg and 50 AED for a package between 3 and 12kg.

How much does a bullet delivery cost?

With a bullet delivery, we will deliver a package directly from the sender to the receiver. It costs 50 AED for a delivery within the same emirate and 190 AED for a delivery between two different emirates. To schedule your bullet delivery, please call us at 04 801 8126.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

You can get same-day delivery under our standard rates if the pickup is scheduled for 09:00 – 12:00 and the delivery is scheduled for 15:00 – 19:00 or 19:00 – 23:00, or if the pickup is scheduled for 12:00 – 15:00 and the delivery for 19:00 – 23:00. Additionally, both the sender and recipient must be located in Dubai. Otherwise, please try our bullet delivery service.

Do you travel to remote areas?

Yes, but we only provide pickup and delivery service on Saturdays. To check if a location is classified as remote, enter the address the app and see which time slots appear. If the only pickup times shown are for Saturday, the area is remote.

Can you collect cash from a recipient?

Yes, we offer a cash-on-delivery service. When placing your order, just click the box marked “Collect cash from receiver?” and enter the amount that you would like us to collect.

How do I get my cash back?

We will deliver the cash we receive directly to you for 20 AED. If you use the service frequently, we can set up a weekly delivery or schedule the delivery for when the cash has accumulated to a certain amount. Alternatively, we can set up a weekly or monthly bank transfer for no extra charge.

If you have other questions about our service, send us an email at support@fetchr.com or call us at 04 801 8126.
